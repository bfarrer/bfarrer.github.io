Signature case studies

Situation

The urgent care business lost its CFO mid-cycle, leaving leadership without reliable clinic-level performance visibility.

Action

Stepped into a head-of-finance capacity, built the analytical framework and reporting infrastructure to evaluate demand trends, and matured the organization's approach to clinic-level analysis.

Result

Delivered data-driven recommendations on clinic closures and hours reductions that leadership acted on, stabilizing performance management through the transition.

Situation

De novo and acquisition performance lived in disconnected datasets across brands, making vintage comparisons and benchmarking slow and unreliable.

Action

Designed and built a dynamic cohort model integrating the disparate sources, supporting de novo analysis, vintage performance tracking, and acquisition benchmarking, alongside a consolidated five-year cross-brand projection model.

Result

Gave leadership a single analytical backbone for evaluating new units and targets, and supported a large-scale corporate refinancing process.

Situation

Marketing spend exceeding $300M was fragmented across multiple dental brands with limited shared standards.

Action

Led the consolidation of the marketing Center of Excellence, aligning brands and bringing analytical rigor to spend and performance.

Result

Created a consolidated function and decision framework that supported executive decision-making across the portfolio.
